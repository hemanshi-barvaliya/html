About Us

First platform that associates helpers and the needy directly

Helpers Family is a platform where we make connecting with our fellows easy so that their lives can be improved. We strive to create a bond between donors and the needy to minimize the levels of hierarchy. The lesser the complexity, the easier to carry out things. We have been dealing with different categories where the needy can request help; for example, Educated Human, Everyone Healthy, No One Hungry, and Specially-Abled Angels.

More About

Know Helpers Family

Helpers Family is an online platform where donors meet needful people; talk to them, and even meet them and help them without any third-party involved. Our platform is dedicated to donors and the needy. It is a protected platform where the donor can decide whom to help. We just act as a medium to link people who wish to donate to help others.
